About The Chat-on-Diary

Ever thought of writing diary?
Want to keep track of your feelings through out the day and everyday ?
How about 'You Chatting with Yourself' ?

You just start feeding this app as and when you feel like. Do it from your laptop, PC, Tab and even the Smart Phone. You just start feeding this application and we will keep track of what you felt and when. At the end of the year/month/day, you can come back and have a look at what you did in the last month/year/day as if you read a book.

No Registration required for using this application. Just sign in with Google and prove your identity. This application will not know/get any of the information from your Google accounts except your user-id. This applicaiton does not require you to register, create yet another user-id/password. Just sign in with Google and start chatting over a Diary.

Your Chat-Diary is Private and Secure for yourself. No one else can access yours.
